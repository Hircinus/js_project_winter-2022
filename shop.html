Amazonians

  • Species: Psilocybe Cubensis
  • Source: Amazon rainforest

    • Amazonians, aka PE or PES Amazonians, are a long-favourite mushroom of the people.
    They grow naturally in Central and South American regions, and have been used in indigenous ceremonies for thousands of years.
    These groups use mushrooms to reach spiritual enlightenment.
    The effects are often described as potent, on the cerebral side more than hallucinogenic.


Blue meanies

  • Species: Psilocybe Cubensis
  • Source: Sub-tropical climates (The Americas, Africa, Southeast Asia, Australia)

    • Blue Meanies Psilocybe Cubensis are named after a strain of Psilocybe Cyanescens of the same name.
    Psilocybe Cyanescens are two times stronger than Cubensis strains, and this strain of Cubensis is named after the Psilocybe Cyanescens variety as an ode to its potency.
